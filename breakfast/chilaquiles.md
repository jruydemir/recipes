Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 4 roma tomatoes
* [ ] 4-8 guajillo peppers sub maybe a morina or new mexico
* [ ] 2 cloves garlic (skin on)
* [ ] 1/2 medium onion
* [ ] 2 tsp. chicken bouillion
* [ ] salt
* [ ] pepper

Preparation
===========
1. In a med pot boil tomatoes, peppers and garlic. 
2. Once the tomatoes are soft, remove them and the garlic. Leave the peppers off heat.
3. Blend the tomatoes, garlic (skins off), and peppers. Add onion, boullion, salt, and pepper with 1-2 cups water.
4. Fry in a bit of canola oil.

Notes
=====
Serve with eggs, thin shaved beef, refried beans.

Top with queso fresca, crema, avacado, cilantro

Maybe try searing the onion on a dry pan.
