Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 6 day-old everything bagels, cubed (about 12 cups)
* [ ] 2 bell pepper, diced
* [ ] 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
* [ ] 1 lb sausage
* [ ] 1 and 1/2 cups shredded cheese
* [ ] 12 large eggs
* [ ] 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard*
* [ ] 2 cups whole milk
* [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
* [ ] 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
* [ ] 6 ounces full-fat brick cream cheese, diced
* [ ] 6 green onions, sliced

Preparation
===========
1. Brown sausage well.
2. In greased baking pan, mix bagels, bell peppers, tomatoes, sausage, and most of the cheese.
3. In a bowl whisk eggs, mustard. Add milk, salt, and pepper. Pour mixture over bagel mixture.
4. Sprinkle cream cheese, cheese, and green onion evenly over the mixture.
5. Preheat oven to 375. Allow cassarole to absorb liquids. Bake in oven for 45 minutes.

Notes
=====

May be too much mustard.
