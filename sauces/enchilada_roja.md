Pretty good enchilada sauce. Works well with chicken, pork or, beef.

Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 10 dried guajillo chiles stems removed and deveined
* [ ] 4 chile de arbol stems removed and deveined
* [ ] 4 roma tomatoes
* [ ] 1/2 onion
* [ ] 2 garlic cloves
* [ ] 1/4 tsp cumin
* [ ] 2 tsp salt (or chicken bouillon)
* [ ] 2 cup water
* [ ]  Coija

Preparation
===========
1. Soak the Chiles in just enough hot water to cover them. Weigh them down to keep them submerged.

2. Roast the tomatoes, onion, and garlic until slightly browned on the outside

3. Add all ingredients to the blender and puree until smooth (2-4 minutes).

4. Strain the sauce in a medium mesh strainer
