Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 1 can sweetened condensed milk
* [ ] 1 1/2 cups ice cold water
* [ ] 1 box instant vanilla pudding
* [ ] 3 cups heavy cream
* [ ] 4 cups barely ripe bananas
* [ ] 1 box nilla wafers

Preparation
===========
1. Beat together milk and water for 1 minute. Add pudding mix, and beat for 2 minutes.
2. Cover and rest in fridge for minimum of 4 hours or overnight.
3. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks.
4. Gently fold whipped cream into pudding.
5. In dish layer wafers, bananas, then pudding. Repeat as many layers as you'd like for the dish.

Notes
=====

Can sprinkle broken wafers on top.
