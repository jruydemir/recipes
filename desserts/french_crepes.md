Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 230g flour
* [ ] 3 eggs
* [ ] 1/4 tsp salt
* [ ] 1 tsp vanilla, optional
* [ ] 1 tsp sugar
* [ ] 250 ml milk
* [ ] 250 ml water
* [ ] 2 tbsp butter, melted

Preparation
===========
1. Mix eggs, salt, vanilla, sugar, milk, water, and butter. 
2. Add flour, whisk until there are no lumps. Cover and rest in fridge for at least 30 min.
3. Cook in a pan.
