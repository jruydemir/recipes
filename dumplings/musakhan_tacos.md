Perfect Marriage of Arab cuisine into a mexican tortilla. It's REALLY REALLY good.

Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 1 tsp salt
* [ ] 1/2 tsp coriander
* [ ] 1/2 tsp paprika
* [ ] 1/2 tsp cumin
* [ ] 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* [ ] 1/4 tsp cardamom
* [ ] 1/2 tsp black pepper
* [ ] 2 tbsp olive oil
* [ ] 1 1/2 lbs chicken breast, butterflied

* [ ] 2 red onions, sliced
* [ ] 1 tbsp sumac
* [ ] 1 tsp salt

* [ ] 1 cup yogurt
* [ ] 1 clove garlic
* [ ] 1/2 tsp salt
* [ ] 1/4 cup water

* [ ] 3 tbsp olive oil
* [ ] 1/2 brick of akawi cheese, crumbled
* [ ] 24 corn tortillas

Preparation
===========
1. Mix the salt, coriander, paprika, cumin, garlic, cardamom, black pepper and olive oil in a medium bowl. Add chicken breast and coat well. Cover and place in fridge for 1-4 hours. When time is up, start the next step.
2. In a medium bowl, mix red onions, sumac and salt. Set aside for 20 minutes.
3. In a small bowl mix yogurt, grated garlic, salt, and water until smooth.
4. Preheat the airfryer or grill. 
5. In an airfryer or grill, cook the chicken. Remove the chicken and let rest covered for 5 minutes.
6. In a pan over medium high heat add the onions. Saute until moderately crispy.
7. Shred the chicken breast.

To Assemble: Heat the corn tortillas in the pan used for the onions. Flip over and sprinkle the cheese over the tacos. Cover and add a few drops of water to let the cheese melt slightly. Place on plate and form tacos laying chicken, onions, and healthy drizzle of yogurt.

Notes
=====

This is a 1st draft, but it went pretty well. But the chicken was a tad dry. If it's still dry after letting rest. It may be worth boiling the breast instead to retain moisture.

Also try with some fresh salsa.
