Masa
====

* [ ] 1 1/3 cup lard/shortening
* [ ] 3 lbs masa harina
* [ ] 2 tsp salt
* [ ] 4 cups stock (chicken, pork, vegetable)

1. Whip lard in mixer for about 2 minutes
2. Add masa and salt, and mix until sandy.
3. Mix in most of the stock (less if making pork tamales), once wet enough turn as high as you can to whip more air into the masa.
