Simple, tasty curry. Comes together in the time it takes for the rice to cook.

Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 1 tbsp red thai curry paste
* [ ] 1 tbsp fish sauce
* [ ] 1 tsp palm or brown sugar
* [ ] 1 tsp tumeric
* [ ] 400 ml coconut milk
* [ ] 400 ml chicken stock
* [ ] 10 g fresh grated ginger
* [ ] 1 green bell pepper, diced
* [ ] 1/2 onion, diced
* [ ] 1 carrot, julienned
* [ ] 700 g fresh cod
* [ ] fresh coriander
* [ ] chile oil and lime wedges

Preparation
===========
1. In hot pot, add curry paste, fish sauce, sugar, and tumeric. Mix quickly for 30 seconds then add coconut milk, and stock. Bring to a simmer for 15 minutes.
2. Add vegetables, let simmer for 10 minutes.
3. Add cod, and simmer for 6-8 minutes.
4. Serve with rice, coriander, chile oil and lime wedges.
